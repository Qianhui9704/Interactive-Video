You will cooperate with your partner to learn the mechanism of sound and violin. You two will:

  • Individually watch a background introduction video together.

  • Be assigned to watch different parts of a video.

  • After watching your own video clip, each person is going to receive a formative assessment to make sure you understand the content.

  • Teach your partner the part you just learned.

  • Take an individual test which will cover both parts.
